description: Guide for ensuring the art content of a game is appropriate for the game's theme and narration, and consistent across the game.
---

To ensure the art content of a game is appropriate for the game's theme and narration, and consistent across the game, follow this process:
1. Understand the game theme and design by reading the documents in $0.
2. Review the implementation and identify the issues that:
  - are not consistent with the game theme and design;
  - are not appropriate for the game's narration;
  - are not consistent across the game;
  - are not proper Game UI, either the style or the functionality.
  - are not easy-to-understand UI for players.
  - are not easy to use UI for players.
  - are not aethetically pleasing.
3. Ask a developer agent to fix the issues identified in step 2, and provide them with the necessary information and resources to do so.
